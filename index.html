● Features

Like CI/CD,
but for your ops.

Build automated workflows that connect messaging channels, AI, orders, payments, and delivery into one seamless pipeline.

💬
Omnichannel Inbox
Handle conversations from WhatsApp, Instagram, SMS, and email in a single unified inbox with smart routing and AI handoff controls.
⚡
Visual Flow Builder
Drag-and-drop automation canvas. Connect triggers, AI steps, conditions, and actions like a pipeline diagram — no code needed.
🧠
AI That Actually Works
AI handles end-to-end customer conversations, qualifies leads, takes orders, and hands off to humans exactly when needed.

Payments Built In

Call a rider, track deliveries, update customers in real time, and handle failed deliveries — all without leaving HanDl.
📊
Analytics & Reports
